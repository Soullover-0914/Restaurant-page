Seasonal Menu Highlights: Fresh Flavors of Summer!

At byteXL New Flavours, we believe in celebrating the bounty of each season. Our chefs are constantly innovating to bring you dishes that feature the freshest, most vibrant ingredients available. This summer, we're particularly excited about some new additions that capture the essence of the warmer months.

Refreshing Appetizers

Beat the heat with our new Chilled Cucumber and Mint Soup, a light and invigorating start to your meal. Also, don't miss our Heirloom Tomato and Burrata Salad, showcasing the sweetness of local tomatoes with creamy burrata and a hint of basil pesto.

Main Course Delights

Our Pan-Seared Halibut with Lemon-Dill Sauce is a must-try, perfectly flaky and bursting with fresh flavors. For our vegetarian guests, the Grilled Asparagus and Mushroom Risotto, made with Arborio rice and a touch of truffle oil, offers a comforting yet refined experience.

These dishes are available for a limited time, so make sure to visit us soon to experience the fresh tastes of our summer menu! You can view our full menu on the menu page.

We're passionate about bringing you the best of what each season has to offer.
